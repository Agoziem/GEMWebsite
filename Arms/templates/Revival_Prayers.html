Revival and Restoration

To see people, nations, and the world revived by the presence and power of God.

Our Motivating Belief

Prayer is essential to fulfilling the Kingdom purpose of intimacy, glory, and dominion.
Jesus said – Men always ought to pray and not lose heart (Luke 18:1).
Paul said – Pray without ceasing (1 Thessalonians 5:17).
James said – The earnest, heartfelt, continued, prayer of a righteous man makes tremendous power available, dynamic in its working (James 5:16b AMPC).

Mission

To power the birth, growth, and sustenance of revival in our lives, nation, and world through prayers; as we connect with God, ignite our spirit,and intercede for revival, in our lives, nation, and world.

Task

Connect, Ignite, Intercede

Connect with God & Ignite our human spirit: through repentance, thanksgiving, praise, worship, acknowledgement of God’s divine nature, affirming our divine nature, and pressing into God’s will in prayer.

Interceding for personal revival, for people, nations, and the world: Speaking over every sphere of our life. Speaking over specific people, groups, spheres, nations, countries, and the world.

Goal

To see Father’s will done in us, in people and in nations.

To see Jesus’ glory revealed in us, in people and in nations.

To see the Holy Spirit have His way in us, in people and in nations.

To see People & Nations overwhelmed, revived, transformed, and restored by the presence & power of God.

Through Prayer

We Connect, We Ignite, We Intercede, We Cause Changes.

We Connect with God and We Ignite our Spirit through:
  • Repentance by the blood of Jesus and the Spirit of God.
  • Thanksgiving, praise, & worship.
  • Acknowledgement of God’s divine nature in prayer.
  • Affirming our divine nature in prayer.
  • Affirming God’s will in prayer.
  • Speaking the language of the Spirit.

We Intercede for Personal Revival, for People, Nations and the World.
  • Interceding for personal revival, for people, groups, spheres, society, nations, and the world.
  • Interceding for Kingdom revival and restoration in the seven spheres of life.
  • Interceding for Kingdom revival and restoration in the seven spheres of society.
  • Interceding for Kingdom revival and restoration in the nations and the continents of the world.
